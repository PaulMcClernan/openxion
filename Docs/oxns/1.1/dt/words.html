Data Types

words

Supported By

  • XION Scripting Language Standard (1.0)
  • OpenXION (1.0, 1.1, 1.2, 1.3, 1.4)
  • OpenXION Standard Module (1.0, 1.1, 1.2, 1.3, 1.4)
  • HyperTalk (2.2)

Descriptors

  • index range descriptor: put words 2 through 7 of steve into bill
  • ordinal range descriptor: put the third through eighth words of steve into bill
  • mass descriptor: put the words of steve into bill

Description

The words data type is a chunk type of string that represents a chunk of text delimited by one or more white space characters.

Note

Accessing a word chunk that is out of range returns empty. Modifying a word chunk that is out of range prepends or appends to the parent string.
