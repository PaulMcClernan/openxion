Data Types

paragraph

Supported By

  • XION Scripting Language Standard (1.0)
  • OpenXION (1.0, 1.1, 1.2, 1.3, 1.4)
  • OpenXION Standard Module (1.0, 1.1, 1.2, 1.3, 1.4)

Descriptors

  • index descriptor: put paragraph 12 of steve into bill
  • index range descriptor: put paragraph 2 through 7 of steve into bill
  • ordinal descriptor: put the fifth paragraph of steve into bill
  • ordinal range descriptor: put the third through eighth paragraph of steve into bill
  • name descriptor: put paragraph "andy" of steve into bill

Description

The paragraph data type is a chunk type of string that represents a chunk of text delimited by one or more line breaks.

Note

Accessing a paragraph chunk that is out of range returns empty. Modifying a paragraph chunk that is out of range prepends or appends to the parent string.

Synonym

para
