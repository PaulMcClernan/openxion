Data Types

sentences

Supported By

  • XION Scripting Language Standard (1.0)
  • OpenXION (1.0, 1.1, 1.2, 1.3, 1.4)
  • OpenXION Standard Module (1.0, 1.1, 1.2, 1.3, 1.4)

Descriptors

  • index range descriptor: put sentences 2 through 7 of steve into bill
  • ordinal range descriptor: put the third through eighth sentences of steve into bill
  • mass descriptor: put the sentences of steve into bill

Description

The sentences data type is a chunk type of string that represents a chunk of text starting with a non-white space character and ending with a period, exclamation mark, or question mark followed by zero or more other non-white space characters.

Note

Accessing a sentence chunk that is out of range returns empty. Modifying a sentence chunk that is out of range prepends or appends to the parent string.

Synonym

sents
