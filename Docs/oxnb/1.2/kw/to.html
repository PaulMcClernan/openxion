Other Keywords

to

Supported By

  • XION Scripting Language Standard (1.0)
  • OpenXION (1.0, 1.1, 1.2, 1.3, 1.4)
  • OpenXION Base (1.0, 1.1, 1.2, 1.3, 1.4)
  • HyperTalk (2.2)

Syntax

datatype index to index

ordinal to ordinal datatype

Example

get words 12 to 13 of steve

get fifth to ninth files of folder myPath

get disks 1 to 4

Description

The to keyword is used in descriptors to specify a range of indexes or ordinals.

Synonyms

through, thru
