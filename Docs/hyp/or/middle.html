Ordinals

middle

Supported By

  • XION Scripting Language Standard (1.0)
  • OpenXION (1.0, 1.1, 1.2, 1.3, 1.4)
  • OpenXION Standard Module (1.0, 1.1, 1.2, 1.3, 1.4)
  • HyperTalk (2.2)

Examples

put the middle word of "It was the turtle, not I, who spilled the beans." -- prints "I,"

put the mid item of "It was the turtle, not I, who spilled the beans." -- prints " not I"

Description

A special ordinal corresponding to the middle, or median, value of a set of values. If the number of values is even, the middle ordinal favors the second, or latter, of the two middle values.

Synonym

mid
